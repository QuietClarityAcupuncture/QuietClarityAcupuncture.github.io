Acupuncture

Zero Balancing

Zero Balancing (ZB) is a hands-on bodywork system designed to align your energy body with your physical structure. Simple yet powerful, it focuses on your whole person, even when addressing specific needs. The Zero Balancing process generally takes between 30 and 40 minutes and is performed when you are fully clothed.
